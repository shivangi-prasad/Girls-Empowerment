Women's day '21 - Online Event

How we celebrated Women's day
in online mode

We conducted 3 workshops, of 1 hour each in collaboration with the GirlScript Foundation.
Khushi Garg was chosen as the impact maker by GirlScript Foundation and she managed the entire event.

The topics of the workshops were :

  • Canva
  • Wordpress
  • Git and Github

3 mentors were invited for the same, who conducted the sessions.
Students made mini projects during the workshops and submitted later.
Around 30-40 girls attended the event, in online mode.
